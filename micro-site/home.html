Featured Sites

Cedar Falls

2 Miles

Cedar Falls is a breathtaking 95-foot waterfall nestled within the park. Whether you hike to the falls or simply enjoy the views from above, Cedar Falls is a must-see for any visitor.

Rock House Cave

.25 Mile

Rock House Cave is a unique geological formation with ancient Native American rock art. Explore the cave and learn about the park's rich cultural history.

Bear Cave

.25 Mile

Explore massive sandstone boulders forming narrow passageways and rock shelters, showcasing the incredible forces of nature.

Stout's Point

5 Miles

Stout's Point, located on Petit Jean Mountain's East Brow, offers a serene overlook with breathtaking views of the Arkansas River Valley and a chance to reflect on the romantic legend of Petit Jean.

Our Passion at Petit Jean

At Petit Jean State Park, the soul of Arkansas reveals itself in stunning landscapes and timeless stories. From the majestic Cedar Falls plunging 95 feet into the canyon below, to the mystical Rock House Cave adorned with ancient pictographs, this park is a haven for nature lovers and history enthusiasts alike.

Whether you're walking the scenic Seven Hollows Trail, marveling at the panoramic views from Stout's Point, or enjoying the serene beauty of Cedar Creek Canyon, Petit Jean State Park offers an unforgettable journey through nature's masterpiece.
