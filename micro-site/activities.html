Popular Activities

Hiking Trails

Discover miles of scenic trails winding through diverse landscapes. From easy strolls to challenging hikes, there's a path for everyone.

Guided Tours

Join expert guides to learn about the park’s natural and cultural history, including unique geology, flora, and fauna.

Wildlife Watching

Observe a variety of wildlife, including deer, birds, and other native species in their natural habitat.

Why Choose Petit Jean?

Petit Jean State Park is a haven for outdoor lovers, providing breathtaking views, rich history, and immersive experiences in nature.

Explore rugged trails, stunning waterfalls, and ancient rock formations that tell stories from generations past.
