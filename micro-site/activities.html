Activities at Petite Jean State Park

From hiking and fishing to guided tours and wildlife watching, Petite Jean State Park offers a wide range of activities to suit every visitor. Check out some of the exciting activities you can enjoy:

Hiking Trails: Explore the Wilderness

Discover miles of scenic trails that wind through the park's diverse landscapes. From easy strolls to challenging hikes, there's a trail for every skill level.

Posted by Park Ranger

Guided Tours: Learn from the Experts

Join our knowledgeable guides for tours that explore the park's natural and cultural history. Learn about the unique geology, flora, and fauna of the area.

Posted by Park Ranger

Wildlife Watching: Observe Nature

Spot a variety of wildlife as you explore the park. Keep an eye out for deer, birds, and other animals that call Petite Jean State Park home.

Posted by Park Ranger
